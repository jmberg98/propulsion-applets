Gridded Ion Engine — Acceleration

Stage 2

The second stage of the gridded ion engine is applying an electric field to particles that have drifted past the ionization chamber into the accelerating grids. A voltage difference between the grids creates the field that extracts ions from the plasma and accelerates them to high exhaust velocity, producing thrust.
