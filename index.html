MIT Propulsion Applets

Interactive pages with simulations and derivations covering the fundamentals of in-space propulsion. Topics include mission analysis, introductory plasma physics, and electric propuplsion devices. Applets are designed to be self-contained, though working through earlier material makes later applets more intuitive. Aimed toward undergraduate students with a background in elementary physics and calculus, but learners of all backgrounds are welcome!
